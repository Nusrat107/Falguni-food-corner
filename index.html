specialist of Naga Win

Spice up your meal with our fiery naga wings, coated in a bold, mouthwatering sauce that packs a serious punch. Perfect for those who crave heat and flavor in every bite!

Calling all spice lovers! Our specialist naga wings are a fiery masterpiece, crafted with the perfect blend of intense heat and bold flavors. Each wing is generously coated in our signature naga sauce, delivering a thrilling kick that’s not for the faint-hearted. Served with cooling dips to balance the heat, these wings are a must-try for anyone seeking an unforgettable culinary adventure. Get ready to ignite your taste buds!

Why is the Chicken Meatbox Special?

Satisfy your cravings with our chicken meatball box, featuring juicy, flavorful meatballs paired with your favorite sides. It’s a hearty, delicious meal that’s perfect for any time of day!

Our premium chicken meat box offers fresh, hygienic, and expertly cut pieces, ready for cooking. Each pack is carefully sealed to maintain freshness and quality. Our chicken momos are crafted with tender, flavorful chicken and a blend of aromatic spices. Each momo is carefully wrapped in a soft, thin dough and steamed to perfection. Made with fresh, high-quality ingredients, they offer a juicy and satisfying bite. Enjoy a delicious and authentic momo experience in every pack!

Chicken Fry

Crispy on the outside, tender and juicy on the inside—our chicken fry is packed with bold flavors. Perfectly seasoned and golden-fried for a delicious crunch in every bite!

Our chicken fry is expertly seasoned with a perfect blend of spices for an irresistible flavor. Coated in a crispy, golden batter, each piece delivers a satisfying crunch with every bite.
